Who We Are

We are a 501c3 non-profit organization formed by concerned residents and conservationists of the Valley in 2002. Our goal is to protect the valleyâ€™s history, agricultural heritage, and natural resources.

Our Goals

  • To improve connections and communications with homeowners, landowners, and the community regarding conservation and land preservation.
  • To protect open space and environmentally sensitive areas.
  • To strengthen our Board to prepare future leaders.
  • To provide support to and community involvement in the Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge.

Mission Statement

The Friends of Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge is dedicated to the conservation and enhancement of the natural and cultural resources, rural character, and scenic beauty of the Cherry Valley area.
